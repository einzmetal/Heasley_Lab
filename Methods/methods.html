  • Western Blotting
    • Make SDS gel (7-12%)
    • Load samples in MKLB with 5X loading buffer added
    • Run overnight ~10mA overnight
    • Transfer to Nitrocellulose paper at 400mA for 3-4hr
    • Block for 1hr in Blocking buffer
    • Add Primary Ab (often at 1000x), place at 4C on rocker overnight
    • Wash 3x for 5min each in TBST
    • Place in Blocking buffer with Secondary Ab (2000x) on rocker for 1hr
    • Repeat wash step
    • Incubate with Classico or Crescendo reagent for 2-3min
    • Place in clear sleeve and in dark room, place film over blot for 10s to 10min, depending on intensity
    • If probing for another Ab, strip in Stripping buffer at 50C for 30min
    • Wash 3x for 15min each
    • Repeat blocking step and proceed as before
